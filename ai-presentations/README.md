# ai-presentations

## Overview
This directory contains a collection of **AI-related presentations** created for various purposes, including academic work, self-study, demonstrations, and knowledge sharing.

The presentations cover **diverse topics in Artificial Intelligence** and are not limited to a single domain, framework, or application area.

## Purpose
The purpose of this directory is to store:
- Presentations on AI concepts, methods, and applications  
- Slides prepared for lectures, seminars, or coursework  
- Introductory and advanced overviews of AI-related topics  
- Experimental or exploratory presentations created during learning  

## Content Structure
Each presentation may include:
- Slide decks (e.g., `.pptx`, `.pdf`)
- Accompanying notes or explanations
- Diagrams, figures, and visualizations
- References to datasets, papers, or tools

Presentations are **independent** and may vary in depth, scope, and technical complexity.

## Topics
Topics may include, but are not limited to:
- Machine Learning
- Neural Networks
- Deep Learning
- Optimization Algorithms
- Data Analysis
- AI in Engineering Applications
- Ethical and Practical Considerations of AI

## Notes
This directory is intended as a **personal archive and reference**.  
The level of detail and polish may vary depending on the original purpose of each presentation.
